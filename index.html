Bio

Hello!

I'm Coach Elmar. I have accumulated a wealth of experience as the Head Coach of top-level NCAA D1 men's soccer teams for over 30 years. I have a degree in physical education from one of the best universities in Germany, and I served in the German Air Force before moving to the United States. With my cumulated experience, I can help you to address the multitude of challenges that daily life can present, whether it be related to athletics, school, professional, or personal life.

Coach Elmar is certified as a Life Coach through the International Coach Federaction (ICF).
